Crank and File is a collection of critical design research led by [Oliver Bates](http://oliverbates.co.uk) and [Ben Kirman](http://ben.kirman.org).

## The Concept

Crank and File is website and landing point for mine and Ben's shared critical design work. I'll be cross posting practically everything that goes over there on my site. 

The title comes from word play of "rank and file" and bicycle component the "crank". The crank speaks to the start of our research journey together working with cycle couriers to design better and more desirable futures.

Thanks to [Tony Pickering (Pick Art)](https://www.pick-art.co.uk) for a great logo!
